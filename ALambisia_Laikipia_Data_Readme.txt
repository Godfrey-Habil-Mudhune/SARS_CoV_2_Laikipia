1. PROJECT 
------------  
Title:  Temporal Distribution and Clinical Characteristics of the Alpha, Delta and Omicron SARS-CoV-2 Variants of Concern in Laikipia, Kenya: Institutional and Community-Based Genomic Surveillance.
Dates: 24th April to 31st December 2021
SSC/Protocol No:SERU #4035
Description of the related research project: 

This study aimed at describing the temporal distribution of the SARS-CoV-2 VOC lineages observed in 97 patients who were either attended at LHS Nanyuki or identified through such institutional and community-based surveillance strategies, and understand the clinical characteristics and outcomes associated with the identified VOCs.

Funding organisation:  Wellcome Trust.
Grant no.: Wellcome Trust (220985) and NIHR (17/63/82 (PI Prof. James Nokes) and 16/136/33 (PI Prof. Mark Wooolhouse))
2. DATASET 
------------  
Manuscript Title: Temporal Distribution and Clinical Characteristics of the Alpha, Delta and Omicron SARS-CoV-2 Variants of Concern in Laikipia, Kenya: Institutional and Community-Based Genomic Surveillance.
Dataset Title: Replication Data for: Temporal Distribution and Clinical Characteristics of the Alpha, Delta and Omicron SARS-CoV-2 Variants of Concern in Laikipia, Kenya: Institutional and Community-Based Genomic Surveillance.
Description: 
This dataset is part of a study that investigated the genomic epidemiology of SARS-CoV-2 in Kenya. This dataset contains results of samples positive for SARS-COV-2 by real-time PCR for the period  24th April to 31st December 2021 in Laikipia Kenya. Aside from the samples tested and results of the tests, it also contains the patientsâ€™ sociodemographic characteristic, cycle threshold and symptoms.The dataset is used to generate new SARS-CoV-2 genome sequences from RT-PCR positive samples collected in Laikipia Kenya. 
Publication Year:2022.
